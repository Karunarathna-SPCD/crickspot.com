      ICC Awards-2022 

      The ICC Awards, also known as the International Cricket Council Awards, is an annual event organized by the International Cricket Council (ICC) to recognize and honor outstanding performances in international cricket. The awards were first introduced in 2004 and have since become a prestigious accolade in the world of cricket.
